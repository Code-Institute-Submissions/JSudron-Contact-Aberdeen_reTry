ABOUT

Music for the underground...Clubbing for the counter culture!!!

Founded in 2013 by DJ Jonny Kennedy, Contact has provided Aberdeen with incredible house music on a weekly basis. What started as a sole residency for Jonny at the Exodus nightclub has gradually expanded, with the addition three further resident DJs and moving to a larger venue; UnderDog. With a growing reputation Contact has been able to bring in top guest DJs such as 'Ross From Friends', 'Denis Sulta' and 'Sven Weisemann' at the monthly 'Contact Presents' events.

Having been on a temporary hiatus, the guys are back and ready to provide the Granite City with multiple club nights and take Contact further afield in Scotland. With a number of quality DJs lined up for 2020 including events in Dundee and Glasgow, it's going to be a huge year.
